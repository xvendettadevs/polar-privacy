Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 08, 2026

Polar Terminal ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension handles information when you use it on the Polymarket website.

1. Information We Collect

To provide its core functionality, Polar Terminal accesses the following information:

  • Website Content: The extension reads market titles and event descriptions on polymarket.com to identify which market is being viewed.
  • User Activity: We monitor interactions with the extension's UI elements (such as clicking the "Polar Scan" button) and page load times to provide timely notifications.

2. How We Use Information

The collected information is used strictly to:

  • Identify the prediction market for analysis.
  • Display relevant processing status and notifications.
  • Improve the user interface and experience of the extension.

3. Data Storage and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. All analysis is performed to provide you with real-time feedback. We do not store your personal browsing history outside of the scope of polymarket.com.

4. Security

We implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect the information processed by the extension.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us via the Chrome Web Store support dashboard.
